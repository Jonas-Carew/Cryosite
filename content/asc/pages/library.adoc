== My Reading List ==

This reading list is primarily focused on books in mathematics on occasionally other academic works. It's ordered alphabetically (not automatically though, message me if there is a mistake) with no regard for quality. It may expand to a full list of fictional works or poetry at some point in the future. I also have not read everything in this list to completion, or even started reading. I plan to include expandable descriptions for every book on this list at some point in the future. If you are curious about any of these books and they aren't available at a local public library, please contact me, I am happy to help find you an affordable source.
